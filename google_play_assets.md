### Full Description (Max 4000 chars)
**KidPoints: The Ultimate Family Reward & Chore Manager**

Transform daily chores into exciting quests and build lasting positive habits with KidPoints. Designed by Vagesh Vaibhav, this premium family utility gives parents a powerful yet simple tool to manage rewards, chores, and point-based incentives in a secure, offline environment.

**Key Features for Parents:**
- **Custom Quests:** Create tailored chores like "Homework," "Reading," or "Cleaning Room" with specific point values.
- **Store Management:** Build a personal family shop where kids can redeem points for "Screen Time," "Ice Cream," or custom rewards.
- **Review Center:** Approve or deny reward requests sent by your children in real-time.
- **Full History:** Track every point earned and spent with a detailed family timeline.
- **Transaction Reversal:** Mistakes happen—easily revert any entry to adjust point balances.
- **Data Privacy:** Your data is YOURS. KidPoints uses local storage only—no cloud tracking, no data sharing.
- **Backup & Restore:** Easily export your family data to a JSON file for safe-keeping or device migration.

**Designed for Kids:**
- **Child Mode:** A simplified, read-only interface where kids can track their progress and request rewards.
- **Fun Avatars:** Choose from a variety of fun characters like Lions, Pandas, and Unicorns.
- **Instant Feedback:** See points grow as chores are completed!

**Why Choose KidPoints?**
Unlike other apps that require monthly subscriptions and complex account setups, KidPoints is a focused, offline-first tool that respects your family's privacy.

*Developed by Vagesh Vaibhav.*
